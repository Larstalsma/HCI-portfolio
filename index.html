My HCI Technologies Portfolio

Greetings! My name is Lars Talsma. This portfolio website is to show my contribution to the Human Computer Interaction (HCI) Technologies 2020 course at The Hague University.
In this portfolio, you'll find four sections representing the four challenges, being: AR & VR, Wearables, Artificial Creatures, and Playful Interaction. Each challenge is divided into five parts: research, tinkering, design & build, showcase and reflect.
Besides the four challenges, there's also a Workshops section in the portfolio, where you can view all of the different workshop assignments, constisting of the following three subjects: AR & VR (A-Frame and Unreal), Arduino and Processing.
Finally, the last section contains the Guest Lecture assignment.

I've built the website myself, so feel free to take a look at the underlying code if you so desire. Enjoy my HCI Technologies portfolio!
